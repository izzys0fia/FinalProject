Essays and Supplementals

College Essay Guy: Personal Statement Guide

Tips for Writing a Great Essay

  • Ask early and choose teachers who know you well.
  • Give them your resume or brag sheet.
  • Send a reminder a week before it's due.
  • Always thank them afterward!

Test Scores

For programs in art, music, architecture, or design, a portfolio shows off your talent. This is a key way to go beyond test scores and show your creative side.

What to Remember

  • Recent and polished work (quality over quantity).
  • Variety in medium or skill if possible.
  • Brief descriptions or titles if requested.
  • Review submission guidelines carefully.
