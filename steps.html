What's Next?

After you’ve made your decision and chosen your college, there are a few important tasks you’ll need to take care of before you start classes. Completing these steps will ensure a smooth transition to your new school and help you get ready for the exciting journey ahead.

  • Complete any remaining paperwork: Make sure all necessary forms, like housing, health records, and financial aid documents, are submitted.
  • Stay on top of deadlines: Check for any final deadlines for things like deposits, housing assignments, and class registration.
  • Prepare for the start of school: Look into any programs or events such as signing up for orientation immediately to help you settle in and meet people before classes begin.
  • Finish your checklist: Review any checklists your school provides to ensure you’ve covered everything before your first day.
